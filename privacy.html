Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

NIFLAOT LLC ("we", "us", or "our") operates the A Bissel Torah browser extension and related services. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our products
  • Subscription Data: If you subscribe, we collect email and payment information through our payment processor (Stripe)
  • Preferences: Your settings (theme, language, timezone) are stored locally in your browser

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process subscriptions and payments
  • To send important updates about our services
  • To improve our products

Data Storage

Your preferences (bookmarks, notes, settings) are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data syncs across your devices if you're signed into Chrome.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe: For payment processing. See Stripe's Privacy Policy
  • ExtensionPay: For subscription management
  • Hebcal & Sefaria: For Jewish content and zmanim data

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Payment information is handled securely by Stripe and never stored on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
